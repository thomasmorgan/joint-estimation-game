Instructions

Before you start playing the game, you'll receive training. In each training round, 3 red lines will appear for 1 second and then disappear. Each red line will have a number next to it. Your goal is to remember all three lines.

​

Two seconds later, you will be told to recreate just one of the lines that you saw as accurately as possible (#1, #2, or #3).

Move the mouse left and right to adjust the size of the blue bar, and then click to enter your response. You will have 2 seconds to respond.

​

​

During training, a gray bar will appear above your guess for 2 seconds to show you the correct answer.

​

You will only be shown this bar during the training phase of the game.
