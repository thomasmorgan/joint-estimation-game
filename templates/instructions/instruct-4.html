Instructions

During the game, you will have the opportunity to earn up to $2.33 in bonuses. We will describe these bonuses in greater detail once you have been paired with a partner.

Remember: You must respond within 60 seconds. If you have not entered your response within that time, your game will time out, and you will only earn the base compensation rate for participation. You will not retain any of the bonuses from any rounds that you have already completed.

If your partner times out, you will earn the base compensation rate for participation and any bonuses you have already earned.
